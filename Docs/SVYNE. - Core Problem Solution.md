### Lead Management

Leads arrive through:

- Phone calls
- Text messages
- Emails
- Social media

- Leads get lost
- Follow-ups are missed
- Revenue opportunities disappear

### Scheduling

Appointments and jobs are managed manually.

Result:

- Double bookings
- Miscommunication
- Delays

---

### Customer Information

Information exists in multiple places.

Result:

- Employees waste time searching
- Customers repeat information
- Errors increase

### Invoicing

Invoices are created manually.

Result:

- Delayed payments
- Forgotten invoices

---

### Reporting

Owners lack visibility.

Result:

- Decisions rely on assumptions
- Problems are discovered too late
