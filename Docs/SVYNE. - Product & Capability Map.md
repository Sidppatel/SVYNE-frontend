# svyne. — Product & Capability Map

What svyne. is, what it does, and how it serves local businesses.

---

## 1. What is svyne.?

svyne. is not an off-the-shelf software platform. It is a modular business operations engine.

We eliminate the chaos of daily operations—saving you hours of administrative work and stopping lost revenue—by consolidating spreadsheets, loose paper tickets, missed calls, and manual invoices into a single, clean, automated dashboard.

Instead of forcing a local business to change how they run to fit a generic piece of software, svyne. is custom-configured around the way they already run their business.

---

## 2. What Does svyne. Actually Do?

At its core, svyne. maps any business operation into three clean phases: Inputs, Systems, and Outputs.

```text
  [ INPUTS ]               [ THE svyne. SYSTEM ]             [ OUTPUTS ]

- Contact/Lead Forms  --->   - Central Kanban Pipeline   --->  - Auto-Text Invoices
- Missing Phone Calls --->   - Automated SMS Relays      --->  - Google Review Links
- Facebook Leads      --->   - Smart Bay/Crew Calendars  --->  - Real-Time Live Status
- Ticket Registrations       - Ticket Ledgers                  - Digital Ticket Delivery
```

*Note: The svyne. system acts as your operational bridge, turning chaotic inputs into automated outputs without any manual data entry.*

### Reusable Core Modules

* **The Communication Bridge:** Replaces manual phone tag with automated SMS triggers. The moment a client’s job status changes (e.g., *Permit Pending* -> *Inspection Scheduled*), the system auto-texts the client.
* **Speed-to-Lead Automation:** Connects to Facebook, website forms, or Google Business profiles. If a lead messages the business, the system instantly texts them back to lock them in before they look for a competitor.
* **One-Screen Scheduling & Dispatch:** A central, drag-and-drop scheduling board that allows solo operators or dispatchers to manage crews, bays, and job sites in real-time, preventing double-bookings and showing field updates instantly.
* **2-Minute Text-to-Pay Invoicing:** Allows field technicians or office staff to trigger an instant SMS payment link upon job completion, helping small businesses get paid in under 120 seconds.
* **Automatic Reputation Booster:** Triggers a polite Google review text request the moment a job is marked "Done" on the dashboard.
* **Ticketing & Registration Engine:** Handles custom booking forms, ticketing inventories, digital ticket generation/delivery via text/email, and a central registration ledger with transaction-based pricing.

## 3. Industries Where svyne. Can Help

Because svyne. is engineered on pre-built, robust operational blocks (CRM, Scheduling, Messaging, Billing, Ticketing), you get the reliability and speed of enterprise software with the exact custom fit of a bespoke build—making it highly adaptable for any business that manages customers, calendars, and payments.

### 🚗 Automotive & Collision Shops (e.g., Thronson Body Shop, L&M Tires)

* **The Headache:** Constant phone calls from customers asking: "Is my car ready? Have the parts arrived? How much is the deductible?"
* **The svyne. Blueprint:** A visual pipeline where staff drag a car from *Teardown* to *Paint* to *Ready for Pickup*. Every drag triggers an automatic text update to the vehicle owner.

### ⚡ Residential & Commercial Trade Contractors (e.g., Bowden Electric, Thompson's Electric)

* **The Headache:** Juggling field schedules, missing inbound job requests while on ladders, and late payments on manual invoices.
* **The svyne. Blueprint:** Mobile quote-to-booking flows, automated crew dispatch updates, and text-to-pay triggers upon completion that eliminate late invoices entirely.

### 🏠 Home & Property Services (e.g., Southern Appeal Landscape, Super Roofers)

* **The Headache:** High competition means losing leads if you don't reply in under 5 minutes; forgetting to ask for Google Reviews to stay ranked #1.
* **The svyne. Blueprint:** Instant text follow-ups for website quote forms and automated post-job review acquisition loops.

### 🎟️ Event Organizers, Local Venues, & Community Groups

* **The Headache:** High platform fees from corporate ticket sites, complex registration sheets, and manual check-ins.
* **The svyne. Blueprint:** Custom ticket booking pages, instant SMS ticket delivery with custom QR codes, check-in dashboards, and transaction-based payment processing.

### 🏋️ Studio, Fitness & Class-Based Businesses (e.g., Gyms, Music Lessons)

* **The Headache:** Getting "free trial" sign-ups to actually show up; chasing down monthly dues manually.
* **The svyne. Blueprint:** Automated text reminders (24-hour and 1-hour before class) and integrated billing pipelines.

### 🩺 Local Professional & Medical Offices (e.g., Chiropractors, CPA/Tax Prep)

* **The Headache:** Administrative burden of intake paperwork, chasing clients for documents, and missed appointments.
* **The svyne. Blueprint:** Automated text links sending digital intake forms to fill out before arrival; document status tracking dashboard.
